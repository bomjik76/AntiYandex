Privacy Policy for AntiYandex Extension

1. General Information

The AntiYandex extension respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our extension.

2. Data Collection

Our extension does not collect or store:

  • Personal user data
  • Browsing history
  • Passwords or credentials
  • Financial information

3. Permission Usage

The extension uses the following permissions:

  • webNavigation - to block access to Yandex websites
  • storage - to store local user settings
  • host_permissions - to block specific Yandex domains

All permissions are used exclusively for the extension's primary function - blocking access to Yandex services.

4. Data Security

We take all necessary measures to ensure the security of your data:

  • All data is stored locally in your browser
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • No analytics or usage statistics are collected

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Remove the extension at any time
  • Clear all locally stored data
  • Revoke extension permissions
